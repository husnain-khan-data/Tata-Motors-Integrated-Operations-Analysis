## 👥 Customer Insights Panel

### 📊 Key Insights

* 🛍️ Retail customers represent the largest customer segment, contributing approximately **39%** of the total customer base.
* ⚠️ A significant portion of memberships are expired, indicating low active dealer/customer engagement within operational programs.
* 📅 Customer activity trends show peak operational engagement during **mid-year** and **November** periods.
* 🌍 Dealer and wholesale customer distribution remains balanced across multiple operational regions.
* 💰 Top dealer/customer transactions contribute a major portion of operational revenue concentration.

### 🎯 Recommendations

* 🔄 Introduce dealer retention and membership renewal strategies to improve active participation.
* 📢 Strengthen customer engagement programs in low-performing operational regions.

## 🚚 Tata Motors Shipment Operations

### 📊 Key Insights

* 🚛 Domestic vehicle logistics operations contribute more shipments than international distribution activities.
* ⚖️ Express and Regular logistics services are utilized almost equally, indicating balanced operational demand.
* 📉 Monthly logistics movement shows a gradual decline toward the end of the operational cycle.
* ⏳ Delivery performance indicates an equal ratio between completed and pending deliveries, highlighting opportunities for dispatch optimization.
* 💹 Transportation cost increases proportionally with shipment load, confirming stable logistics pricing trends.

### 🎯 Recommendations

* ✅ Improve pending dispatch management to increase delivery completion efficiency.
* 🛣️ Optimize major logistics routes to reduce operational transportation costs.

## 💰 Financial Health Monitor

### 📊 Key Insights

* 💵 Total operational revenue remains strong, with payment collections reaching nearly half of total revenue generated.
* 💳 COD payments contribute slightly higher transaction volume compared to card-based payments.
* 🏪 Retail customers generate the highest revenue contribution among all customer segments.
* ⚠️ Outstanding payment amounts fluctuate significantly across monthly operations, impacting financial consistency.
* 📈 Revenue collection trends remain stable during high operational activity periods.

### 🎯 Recommendations

* 🔍 Improve outstanding payment recovery and reconciliation processes.
* 📲 Encourage digital payment adoption to enhance faster revenue collection and financial tracking.
